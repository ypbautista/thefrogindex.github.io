What you can find in the Frog Registry

  1. Elite frog profiles from legendary ponds
  2. Jump performance ratings (lily pad certified)
  3. Wisdom levels measured in ancient croaks
  4. Criminal records (mostly fly-related incidents)
  5. Party frogs and swamp entertainers
  6. Frog conservation and habitat protection info

Welcome to the Official Frog Registry! Not all heroes wear capes—some wear algae and croak at midnight. Here you'll find detailed profiles of the pond's most remarkable residents, including their jumping abilities, wisdom levels, and completely unrelated criminal records.

If you're searching for a trustworthy advisor, a professional party frog, or an academic amphibian, you've come to the right place.

Froglife is a UK conservation charity working to protect frogs, toads, newts, and reptiles. They restore habitats like ponds and wetlands and educate communities about wildlife conservation.
